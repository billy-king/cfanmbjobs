Why Chick-fil-A?

At Chick-fil-A, the Team Member role is more than just a job; it's an opportunity. In addition to working directly for an independent Owner/Operator, Team Members gain life experience that goes far beyond serving a great product in a friendly environment. Team Members are responsible for providing an exceptional dining experience for everyone they serve each day and for ensuring all guests receive signature Chick-fil-A service and food.

Our Benefits

  • Flexible Scheduling: We're closed every Sunday. We understand the importance of work-life balance and family time.

  • Progression and Development: Our unique Checklist Progression System allows you to advance at your own pace. Whether you're looking to learn new skills or aiming for a leadership role, we support your growth every step of the way.

  • Scholarship opportunities: Chick-fil-A can help you financially as you earn your degree

  • Competitive Pay: Starting at $14/hour for full-time positions

  • Vibrant Work Environment: Be part of a lively and fun team in a fast-paced setting where you can positively influence others

  • Employee Discounts: Enjoy discounts on our delicious menu items

  • Mentorship: Learning first-hand from an experienced Operator and Restaurant Leaders
